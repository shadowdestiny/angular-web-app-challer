OUR STORY
Challer
CHALLER was born as a new way to help people entertain themselves, connect with others around the world and engage in a fun and interactive way.
Challer was created by a multicultural and multinational team. Back in 2019, our founders started thinking, researching, and working on a new project that would give the younger generation a new way to meet and interact with people with their same interests.
Seeing how the COVID-19 has affected the world, and paying special attention to the vicissitudes that we all have faced while adapting our lifestyle to the “new normal” and stay at home policy, we decided to dedicate ourselves to this project, and launch Challer.
Challer is the perfect place to challenge the world!
