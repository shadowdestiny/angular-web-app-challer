We do not allow dangerous individuals or organizations to use our platform, in particular, to promote terrorism, crime, or other types of behavior that could cause harm. If in own our opinion there is a credible threat to public safety, we will automatically ban the account and we will cooperate with relevant legal authorities.

Dangerous individuals and organizations are those that commit crimes or cause or promote other types of severe harm. We do not allow the depiction, trade, or promotion of firearms, ammunition, firearm accessories, or explosive weapons and we also prohibit instructions on how to manufacture those weapons.

It is expressly forbidden to post content that: displays firearms, firearm accessories, ammunition, or explosive weapons; that offers, sells, trades, or solicits firearms, accessories, ammunition, explosive weapons, or instructions on how to manufacture them.

We do not allow content that depicts the use or trade of drugs or other controlled substances.

It is expressly forbidden to post content that: displays drugs, drug consumption, or encourages others to make, use, or trade drugs or other controlled substances; sells, offers, trades, or solicits drugs or other controlled substances; provides information on how to buy illegal or controlled substances.

It is expressly forbidden to post content that: promotes phishing; ponzi or pyramid schemes; promotes fixed betting, get-rich-quick schemes, or any other types of scams.
